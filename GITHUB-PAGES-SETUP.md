## Enable GitHub Pages

1. Go to your repository: https://github.com/mahoney-jones/investment-calc
2. Click the **"Settings"** tab
3. Scroll down to **"Pages"** in the left sidebar
4. Under **"Source"**, select:
   - **Branch**: `main`
   - **Folder**: `/ (root)`
5. Click **"Save"**

## Your Live Site

After GitHub Pages is enabled (may take 1-2 minutes), your site will be available at:

**https://mahoney-jones.github.io/investment-calc/**

## Continuous Deployment

- The site automatically updates whenever you push changes to the `main` branch
- Updates typically appear within 1-2 minutes after pushing
- Check deployment status in the repository's **"Actions"** tab

## Deployment Status

You can monitor deployments by:
1. Going to: https://github.com/mahoney-jones/investment-calc/actions
2. Looking for "pages build and deployment" workflows
3. Green checkmark indicates successful deployment
